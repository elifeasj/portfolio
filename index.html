ENTREPRENEUR COMPETITION

NEHMAP

Nehmap

The entrepreneur competition was one of the most challenging and exciting tasks that I had in my 1st semester of Multimedia Design. Together with some fellow students, have carried out.
In the project, we focused on women in the nightlife - It was a topic that was current on the news as well as the social media - #TextMeWhenYouGetHome.
After many thoughts and ideas we chose to designed an app for women who can call for help if they feel unsafe or in danger in the nightlife.

We pitched our idea to the The Foundation for Entrepreneurship and made it to the finals.

ROYAL SANDWICH

Royal Sandwich

2022

E-COMMERCE

DIGIEFS

Digiefs

During my second semester I was introduced to the ecommerce project. In this 4-week themed course, I worked with my own idea for a poster concept and from that I developed a business concept.
I designed my posters in the minimalist abstract forms and sold posters through a webshop that I created myself using WordPress. During the whole process, I also created a visual identity for my webshop, where I chose colors and typography based on their symbolic expression. I used Lean Startup as a development method through a process where I have been through the Lean Startup cycle more than once.

I'm Elif.
Nice to meet you.

Born in 1998 and raised in Copenhagen, Denmark. I'm currently studying AP Graduate in Multimedia Design at Academy of Technologies and Business in Zealand - Campus Køge.

Already as a 13-year-old, I had a lot of interest in photography.
I took a lot of pictures, which I used to edit with basic filters. I used to make GIF's in the age of 15 on Adobe Photoshop without knowing it was a way creative people used to work with in for example social media, ads etc. I found out that I could call myself creative after starting as a Multimedia Designer student and I could see I can use my aptitude to creative stuff, I didn't expect I could. During the time with difficult experience and projects, I became better at improving myself socially and professionally in business.

My first Design Thinking project was in 1st semester student as Multimedia Designer, which helped me to see the combination of my knowledge of people and systems with my desire for problem-solving. The Design Thinking process enables me to realize and create innovative solutions and business models in small and big projects.

I like to see other perspective than mine. This helps me to move with my empathy in situations also with people I work with.
For me, it is important to respect different point of view in everything, even though disagreements may occur.
Therefore, I would say that I move every step with my respect and empathy for people I work with and socialize with.

I enjoy doing difficult projects, and I love to explore my other abilities.
